Development of VR for Macular Degeneration

About the Development of the VR Application

This is an exploratory VR-based visualization project that proposes a new way of digital enhancement. It will be a design project aimed at experts at the Emory Eye Center to help us to optimize the VR interface. We do not yet target clinical trials but aim to create the necessary prototype for a possible pilot study. We will use recently released commercial VR equipment (HTC Vive Pro Eye) to build an experimental set up investigating digital media opportunities for people affected by Macular Degeneration.
