Dr. Michael Nitsche

Associate Prof in Digital Media at the School of LMC, will be responsible for the main work: supervising the graduate student to design the VR visualizations for Macular Degeneration condition as well as advising the interaction design that will allow users to adjust these visualizations for their personal customization.

Dr.Susan A. Primo

Professor of Ophthalmology and Director of the Optometry Services at the Emory Eye Center/ Emory University School of Medicine. Primo will be collaborating in the development of the design for the VR interface as the target expert audience for the first version of the VR design project. Primo has agreed to participate and the project is being presented to their research office.
